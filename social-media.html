Social Media Marketing

Turn scrollers into regulars.

Social media is where your customers spend their downtime — and where they decide which local businesses feel alive, trustworthy, and worth visiting. We create content people actually stop for and manage the daily conversations that turn casual followers into regulars who bring their friends.

Get My Free Audit See Our Work

For a small business, social isn't about going viral. It's about being visibly excellent in your own zip code: showing the work, the people, and the proof, consistently. BrightLocal's consumer research shows people vet local businesses online before ever walking in — your feed is part of that vetting, whether you're posting or not.

How we run your social.

  1. Channel strategy

    We pick the two platforms where your customers actually are and go deep, instead of posting thinly everywhere. A gym lives on Instagram and TikTok; a B2B service wins on LinkedIn and Google posts.

  2. Content engine

    A monthly content calendar built from what converts: proof of work, customer stories, offers, and personality. We shoot, design, and write it — you approve from your phone.

  3. Community management

    Comments answered, DMs turned into bookings, reviews acknowledged. Response speed is a trust signal customers notice and algorithms reward.

  4. Report on what matters

    Follower counts are vanity; we report reach, clicks, bookings, and revenue influenced. If a format isn't earning attention, we kill it and reinvest.

What's included.

Content creation

Scroll-stopping posts, reels, and stories designed and written for your brand — built from your real work, team, and customers, not stock clichés.

Community management

Daily monitoring, comment and DM responses in your voice, and review handling that turns public questions into public proof.

Platform strategy

Deep focus on the two channels that fit your business, with formats and posting cadence chosen from data — not from whatever's trendy this month.

Growth & conversion

Bio-to-booking funnels, offer campaigns, and retargeting-ready audiences that turn attention into appointments and sales.

What it costs.

Social media management runs $1,200 to $3,000 per month depending on platforms, posting volume, and whether we're shooting original content on-site. Paid social amplification can be added through our ads service. As always, the free audit comes first — including an honest read on whether social is even your highest-leverage channel right now.

Questions about social media marketing

How much does social media management cost?

Plans run $1,200 to $3,000 per month depending on platforms and content volume. On-site content shoots and paid amplification are quoted separately in your audit.

Which platforms should my business be on?

Almost never all of them. We pick the two where your buyers actually spend time — and the audit shows you where your competitors are winning or ignoring their audience.

Do I need to appear in the content?

It helps — faces outperform logos — but it's not required. We build content systems around what you're comfortable with, from behind-the-scenes work shots to customer features.

How often will you post?

Typically 3 to 5 feed posts and 5 to 10 stories per week per platform, tuned to what the data says your audience rewards. Consistency beats volume.

Can social media actually drive sales for a local business?

Yes — when it's built for conversion, not applause. Bio funnels, bookable links, offer campaigns, and DM-to-appointment flows are where followers become revenue.

Who writes the replies to comments and messages?

We do, in a voice guide you approve. Anything sensitive gets escalated to you immediately — your reputation is never on autopilot.
