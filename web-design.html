Web Design & Development

Websites built to convert, not just to look good.

Your website is your hardest-working salesperson — it pitches every visitor, at any hour, whether you're open or not. We design and build fast, mobile-first sites that guide visitors toward one clear action: becoming a customer. No bloated templates, no guesswork, just a site engineered around how your buyers actually behave.

Stanford's Web Credibility Project found that 75% of people judge a business's credibility by its website design. For a small business, that means your site is either winning trust in the first few seconds or quietly sending customers to a competitor. Everything we build is measured against one question: does this page make it easier for the right customer to say yes?

How we build your site.

  1. Audit & buyer mapping

    We start with your free audit: current site performance, competitor sites, and how your customers actually search and buy. The result is a page-by-page plan tied to revenue, not a mood board.

  2. Design around one action

    Every page gets a single primary call to action. We wireframe the buyer's path first — hero promise, proof, offer — then apply your brand so the design sells before it decorates.

  3. Build lean and fast

    We hand-build with clean semantic code instead of heavyweight page builders. That's why our sites routinely load in under two seconds on a phone — speed Google's research says directly protects your conversion rate.

  4. Launch, measure, refine

    After launch we track real behavior — where visitors click, stall, and convert — and tune the site in the first 60 days. A website is a system you tune, not a brochure you print.

What's included.

Conversion-first design

Every section earns its place, with clear calls to action and a layout mapped to your buyer's journey. We design the path to purchase before we pick a color.

Fast and mobile-first

Google's benchmark research found 53% of mobile visitors abandon a page that takes over 3 seconds to load. We build lean so your site isn't in that statistic.

SEO-ready foundation

Clean semantic markup, structured data, fast Core Web Vitals, and crawlable content — so search engines and AI assistants can read, rank, and recommend you from day one.

Built to grow

You get an easy-to-update site with room to add pages, landing pages, and campaigns as you scale — no developer required for everyday edits.

Questions about web design & development

How much does a small-business website cost?

Most of our builds land between $4,000 and $12,000 one-time, depending on scope — a lean five-page site sits at the lower end, e-commerce at the upper. Your free audit includes an exact, itemized quote.

How long does a new website take?

Most small-business sites launch in 3 to 6 weeks depending on page count and content readiness. We give you a clear timeline after the free audit.

Do you redesign existing websites?

Yes. We often start from an existing site, keep what's working, and rebuild the rest for speed, clarity, and conversions.

Will I be able to edit it myself?

Absolutely. We build on approachable platforms and hand over a site you can update without needing a developer for every change.

Will my site work on phones?

It's built for phones first. More than half of small-business traffic is mobile, so we design the phone experience before the desktop one — then test both.

Do you handle hosting and maintenance?

We can. Care plans from $150/month cover hosting, security updates, backups, and small monthly changes, so your site never quietly rots.
