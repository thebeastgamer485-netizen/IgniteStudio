Branding & Identity

Become the name people remember.

In most local markets, the best-known business beats the best business. Branding is how a small company punches above its weight: a name, look, and voice that customers recognize, trust, and repeat to their friends. We build identities with strategy underneath — not just a logo file, but a system for being memorable.

Get My Free Audit See Our Work

Good branding pays for itself in every channel. Ads convert better when the brand feels established. SEO content earns more clicks when the name looks credible in search results. Stanford's research on web credibility found people judge trustworthiness by look and feel in seconds — branding is how you win that judgment before you've said a word.

Our branding process.

  1. Positioning before pixels

    We interview you, study your competitors, and find the space only you can own. The strategy document we produce drives every design decision that follows — no taste-based guessing.

  2. Identity design

    Logo system, color palette, and typography built for real-world use: storefronts, vehicle wraps, invoices, social avatars, and the favicon squint-test. Beautiful at billboard size and at 16 pixels.

  3. Voice and messaging

    The words matter as much as the mark. You get a tagline, elevator pitch, and tone-of-voice guide so every email, ad, and Instagram caption sounds like the same confident business.

  4. The launch kit

    Brand guidelines, every file format you'll ever be asked for, and templates for the assets you use weekly — so the brand stays consistent long after handoff.

What's included.

Logo & identity system

A primary logo plus the lockups, marks, and clear-space rules that keep it working everywhere — from a shop sign to a social avatar.

Color & typography

A palette and type system chosen for recognition and legibility, with accessibility contrast checked so your brand works for every customer.

Messaging & voice

Tagline, positioning statement, and tone-of-voice guidance that make your marketing sound like you — consistently, on every channel.

Brand guidelines

A practical usage guide your team (and any future vendor) can actually follow, so the brand doesn't drift six months after launch.

What it costs.

Full brand identity projects run $3,500 to $8,000 one-time depending on scope — a focused logo-and-basics package sits at the lower end, a complete identity with messaging and collateral at the upper. Rebrands that need signage, packaging, or vehicle assets are quoted after the free audit so nothing is missed.

Questions about branding & identity

How much does branding cost for a small business?

Identity projects run $3,500 to $8,000 one-time depending on scope. You'll get an itemized quote after the free audit — including what you can safely skip at your stage.

How long does a branding project take?

Most identity projects run 4 to 8 weeks from kickoff to final files, depending on feedback rounds and how much collateral you need.

I already have a logo — can you work with it?

Often, yes. A brand refresh keeps the recognition you've earned and fixes what's holding you back. The audit will tell you honestly whether refresh or rebuild is the better investment.

What files will I actually receive?

Everything: vector masters, print-ready and web-ready versions, social crops, favicon set, plus the guidelines document. You own all of it outright.

Is branding worth it before I've grown?

A modest, well-built identity early beats an expensive rebrand later — it makes every ad, sign, and page you buy afterwards work harder. We'll size the project to your stage, not our portfolio.

Do you design signage, packaging, or vehicle wraps?

Yes — physical brand touchpoints are often where local businesses win recognition, and we design them as part of the identity system, not an afterthought.
